# Climate Change Risk on Rural Population

This repository contains notebooks and datasets used to assess climate change impacts on the agricultural sector in Indonesia, with a specific focus on rural populations. The assessment follows the IPCC risk framework, integrating exposure, sensitivity, and adaptive capacity.

---

## Result

This dashboard visualizes agricultural climate risk across regions in Indonesia by combining exposure, sensitivity, and adaptive capacity indicators to identify areas most vulnerable to climate change impacts.
